About

C.J. Stroud is a professional American football quarterback for the Houston Texans. He played college football at Ohio State, where he became one of the top quarterbacks in the nation and a Heisman Trophy finalist.

Known for his accuracy, composure, and strong arm, Stroud quickly made an impact in the NFL after being selected in the first round of the 2023 Draft.
